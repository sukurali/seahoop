    Purpose

    SeaHoop’s founder was a basketball lover, like every other basketball player, he has always dreamed of playing in front of millions of people around the globe on television. But his basketball dream was destroyed when he got into an accident which caused him permanent disability in his leg. From elementary school basketball to high school varsity basketball, he has always been frustrated with being unable to predict where and when a pickup basketball game will happen. This has caused him a significant amount of time, and effort. After receiving the news that he is most likely never returning to the sport he loves at a competitive level, he decided to do something for the basketball community in Seattle. The sport that gave him lifelong friends, family, and life lessons.

    Community

    As technology improves day by day, our community relies heavily on electronic products. Most people today chooses socializing on the online world, instead of in person. Our goal is to bring the game we love back to us and our future generations.

    Information

    Gain access to over 100+ basketball court locations in the Greater Seattle Area. SeaHoop provides real-time and future player counts and information. So you can plan ahead, and show up ready to play!

    Digital

    In today’s world pick-up game information is still transferred by word. Or you show up hoping to have enough people to play, but we are changing that. As information becomes digital, is easier to access information and plan ahead accordingly. This is why SeaHoop was born.

    About

    App Details

    SeaHoop began from scratch as an idea drafted from pencil and paper, which services the Greater Seattle and the surrounding area. We are a group of basketball players committed to sharing basketball court information with those who want to play with others. At a time when the media seems to be dividing us, SeaHoop is working toward bringing hoopers together. SeaHoop provides daily court traffic statistics for over 100 different basketball locations in Seattle. Join us, download the app, and get involved in bringing the game we all love to your neighborhood by working together.
